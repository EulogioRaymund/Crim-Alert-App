Afritada

Ingredients

1 pound chicken, cut into serving pieces

2 tablespoons cooking oil

1 onion, chopped

2 cloves garlic, minced

2 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and quartered

2 medium-sized carrots, peeled and cut into chunks

1 red bell pepper, cut into strips

1 green bell pepper, cut into strips

1 cup tomato sauce

1 cup water

1 tablespoon soy sauce

Salt and pepper to taste

Direction

Step 1

Heat the cooking oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic and saut√© until the onions are translucent

Step 2

Add the chicken to the pot and cook until lightly browned on all sides.

Step 3

: Pour in the tomato sauce and water and stir to combine. Let it simmer for a few minutes

Step 4

Add the potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers to the pot. Stir to mix well.

Step 5

Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference

Step 6

Cover the pot and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked and the vegetables are tender.

Step 7

Adjust the sauce consistency by adding more water as needed. Simmer for a few more minutes until the sauce thickens to your desired consistency.

Step 8

Serve the afritada hot with steamed rice. Enjoy this hearty and savory Filipino stew, which is sure to warm your heart and soul.

Tips:

1. Choose the right chicken: The quality of your chicken is the key to a great afritada. Choose chicken that is fresh and good quality.

2. Use a good gravy: The gravy is the most important part of the afritada. Make sure you use cream or coconut milk to give your gravy a rich and creamy texture.

3. Marinate the chicken: Marinate your chicken in a mixture of vinegar, garlic, and ginger before frying it to add flavor.

4. Fry the chicken separately: Fry the chicken separately from the gravy to prevent the chicken from getting tough.

5. Serve it hot: Serve the afritada while it's hot to enjoy its full flavor. A sprinkle of scallions on top adds a nice touch!
